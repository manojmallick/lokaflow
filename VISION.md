# LokaFlow™ — Vision

*by LearnHubPlay BV · Netherlands · March 2026*

---

## The Problem

AI is becoming expensive, rate-limited, and inaccessible.

Power users today pay $110+/month across Claude Pro, ChatGPT Plus, Copilot,
and Perplexity — and still hit rate limits on heavy days. Every query goes
to a data centre. Every document leaves your machine. Every token costs money.

Meanwhile, billions of people are locked out entirely. Not by choice — by
credit cards they don't have, internet bandwidth they can't afford, and
pricing models built for Silicon Valley salaries.

And local models have crossed a threshold: Llama 3.3, Qwen 2.5, and
Mistral 7B running on a modern laptop produce output that is genuinely
useful for the majority of everyday AI tasks. The hardware is ready.
The routing layer is not.

---

## What LokaFlow Is

LokaFlow™ is an intelligent orchestration layer that routes AI tasks to
the right model at the right cost — automatically.

**Local first.** Simple, bulk, and private tasks run entirely on your
hardware using Ollama. No cloud. No cost. No limits. No internet required.

**Cloud when it matters.** Complex reasoning and frontier-quality tasks
escalate to Claude, GPT-4, or Gemini — but only when local cannot do
the job well enough.

**Plan, then execute.** A powerful cloud model plans the work. Local models
execute the steps in bulk. Frontier-quality output at a fraction of the cost.

## Core Beliefs

**1. AI should be a human right, not a subscription service.**
The value AI delivers — learning, productivity, creativity — should not
be gated by income, geography, or credit card access. Local compute is the
equaliser. LokaFlow is the delivery mechanism.

**2. Your data should stay on your machine by default.**
Privacy should not require effort or a premium plan. LokaFlow's PII scanner
intercepts sensitive content before it reaches any cloud API. Your IBAN,
BSN, medical notes, and contracts stay where they belong: on your machine.

**3. Cost should not limit curiosity.**
Rate limits and token budgets change how people think. When local inference
is free and unlimited, users ask more questions, iterate more freely,
and build more ambitious things. Artificial scarcity is a design choice.
We choose not to make it.

**4. The threshold for payment must be fair.**
Companies with 100+ employees and €1M+ revenue have legal teams, finance
teams, and compliance budgets. They can afford €49–999/month. A 150-person
NGO with €200K in grants cannot. Our pricing reflects this reality:
**both conditions must be true to pay.**

**5. Orchestration is the missing layer.**
The hard problem is not building a better model. It is knowing which model
to use, when, for what, at what cost. That routing layer is LokaFlow.

**6. Open by default, sustainable by design.**
LokaFlow is free for individuals and most organisations. The enterprises
that derive commercial value from its capabilities fund development.
This is the model that keeps the project independent and improving.

## The Product Family

All products are modules or modes of ONE codebase. Not separate apps.
Not separate teams. Not separate pricing pages.

### 🌊 LokaFlow — The Infrastructure
*Free for everyone, forever.*
The core routing engine, dashboard, cost analytics, privacy controls,
and model management. This is the product. Everything else extends it.

### 🤖 LokaAgent™ — Automation
*Free for everyone, forever.*
8-stage DAG orchestration pipeline: PromptGuard → ComplexityScorer →
TaskSplitter → ModelMatcher → ContextPacker → ExecutionEngine →
QualityGate → Assembler. Complex multi-step workflows at local cost.

### 🛡️ LokaGuard™ — Compliance
*Module inside LokaFlow. Unlocked for Business and Enterprise tiers.*
DORA Article 11, SOX Section 404, GDPR Article 30 compliance.
Automated audit trail, PDF report generation, PII custom rules,
data residency enforcement. For regulated industries that need to
prove their AI is compliant — not just hope it is.

### 🏢 LokaEnterprise — Corporate Deployment
*Docker deployment mode + admin panel. Enterprise tier.*
On-premise installation inside corporate networks. Air-gapped mode
for zero external data flow. SSO (Entra ID, Google Workspace, SAML).
Department routing policies. White-label capability. Admin panel.

### 🎓 LokaLearn — Education
*Content pack. Free for everyone.*
30+ prompt templates for coding education, essay writing, mathematics,
language learning, research, and exam preparation. Pre-installed for
schools and NGOs. Works offline after setup. Available in 50+ languages
via local Qwen 2.5 multilingual models.
*Future (grant-funded): native Android app for offline mobile access.*

### 🌍 LokaAccess — Global Access Initiative
*Partnership programme. Mobile build 2028.*
AI for people with limited internet. Works on 4-year-old Android phones.
No credit card. No data cost after initial setup. Target: telco and NGO
partnerships to reach populations that current AI tools cannot.

## What We Will Not Build

- A model. We route — we do not train.
- A cloud service that holds your data. LokaFlow runs on your machine.
- A wrapper that calls one API. The value is in the routing logic.
- A freemium trap that hobbles the free tier to force upgrades.
- A product that requires a credit card to be useful.
- A pricing model that charges per query, per seat, or per token.
- Anything that sends user data to improve our own systems.

## Contribution Philosophy

LokaFlow is built in the open. Contributions are welcome under the terms
of the [Contributor License Agreement](./CONTRIBUTING.md).

We merge changes that:
- Improve routing intelligence or accuracy
- Add support for new local or cloud model providers
- Improve privacy, security, or cost transparency
- Fix bugs with clear reproduction steps
- Extend LokaLearn education templates (no CLA required for content packs)

We do not merge:
- Changes that introduce cloud dependencies for core routing logic
- Features that send additional data to external services by default
- PRs without tests for new routing behaviour
- Monetisation features that would limit the free tier
